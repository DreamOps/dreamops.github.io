Tech Talk Tuesday

Tech Talk Tuesday features enterprise-grade webinars from thought leaders throughout the software development technoverse. Check in regularly for the best of the best!

  • For the best in blogs, visit [http://feedly.com/dreamops Planet DreamOps]

== Talks 2016 ==

  • 26-Jul-2016 – Machine learning is a powerful tool for building smarter software. Today we will look at series of videos put together by the Google Developers to learn how we can begin leveraging Machine Learning ourselves. – [https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLOU2XLYxmsIIuiBfYad6rFYQU_jL2ryal Google Machine Learning Recipes]

  • 12-Jul-2016 – Today’s Tech Talk Tuesday will be composed of several videos. With the surge of Pokemon Go players, augmented reality is rapidly becoming a huge part of our lives. Augmented reality can change the way we interact with the devices we have become so dependent on. The talks we will be watching include:
    [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H9ZOpQzjukY A glimpse of the future through an AR headset] [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=frrZbq2LpwI Image recognition that triggers AR]
    ** [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AgMOJC5R4F8 How AR will change sports]

  • 28-Jun-2016 – Today we will watch the second part of the Mastering Lightning Framework session from the TrailheaDX developer conference. Part 2 focuses on building the underlying logic of lightning components with Apex and Javascript. – [https://www.salesforce.com/video/249211/ Mastering Lightning Framework - Part 2]

  • 21-Jun-2016 – Today we will be watching a video on the Salesforce Lightning Framework from the recent Salesforce TrailHeaDX developer conference. The video is the first of a two part series which takes an in depth look at the Lightning Framework. Part 1 focuses on the structure of lightning components, component architecture, and component implementation. – [https://www.salesforce.com/video/249210/ Mastering Lightning Framework - Part 1]

  • 31-May-2016 – This Google Tech Talk is about writing more testable code. Misko Hevery goes over several good practices for writing code that is easier to test. He also discusses why our intuition of testing is often wrong and how we can shift the way we view testing the code we write. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XcT4yYu_TTs How To Write Clean, Testable Code]

  • 10-May-2016 – This video looks at a new part of Heroku Enterprise called Private Spaces. Private spaces offer a dedicated runtime environment for storing data that can be connected with other systems. This video will cover the basics of Private Spaces and go over the benefits of using them with Salesforce. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k9xbH2mcHY4 Diving Into Heroku Private Spaces]

  • 26-Apr-2016 – Lea Verou will break down the syntax used for regular expressions. She begins by going over the basic regex syntax that most of us are familiar with. She then focuses on more advanced regex statements and progressively breaks down the more complicated yet more powerful regex that some of us shy away from. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EkluES9Rvak Demystifying Regular Expressions]

  • 12-Apr-2016 – This Google Tech Talk focuses on the importance of quality API design. Josh Bloch talks about the power of a well-designed API and goes over some best practices to keep in mind when developing your own API. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aAb7hSCtvGw How to Design A Good API and Why it Matters]

  • 22-Mar-2016 – This talk focuses on the Salesforce BigObject and AsyncSOQL pilot features and how they will change the way we analyze massive amounts of data on the Salesforce platform. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UU-BLfbE3bU Unleash the Potential of Big Data]

  • 15-Mar-2016 – This Salesforce presentation goes over the highlights of the Spring ‘16 release. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KEhvrU9YrWQ Salesforce Summer ‘16 Release Highlights]

  • 8-Mar-2016 – Alan Watkins breaks down the physiological barriers that prevent us from performing at our best each day. In this TED talk titled, “Being Brilliant Every Single Day”, he will talk about how the human mind and body respond to different stimuli and go over the ways that we can stay in control no matter what the world throws at us. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fRItG9G1rb4 Being Brilliant Every Single Day by Alan Watkins]

  • 1-Mar-2016 – Rich Hickey discusses the importance of simplicity in software systems and different type of agility that we don’t hear about to often, Architectural Agility. That is, the ability for us to adapt our software to do new things or do old things better. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rI8tNMsozo0 Rails Conf2012 - Simplicity Matters by Rich Hickey]

  • 23-Feb-2016 – This talk focuses on eliminating cognitive biases which suggest that some people are naturally gifted programmers. Angelina Fabbro will talk about ways that we can all grow from intermediate programmers to experts! – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v0TFmdO4ZP0 JavaScript Masterclass by Angelina Fabbro]

  • 26-Jan-2016 – An interesting perspective on how software engineering is taught in universities does not necessarily work or produce high quality software. Glen Vanderburg has recommendations on adopting useful practices and how to further the quality of software that engineers produce. – [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NP9AIUT9nos Lone Star Ruby Conference 2010 Real Software Engineering by Glenn Vanderburg]
