About DreamOps

We are Force.com enterprise developers who want to solve the technical problems we encounter when creating large or complex custom solutions and packages by working with other developers and the Force.com creators.

We care about curating large bodies of Apex code (> 1mb), maintaining large sets of unit tests (> 500), managing legacy data integrations, developing sophisticated Visualforce pages, and creating innovative Salesforce1/Heroku1 applications.

Our goal is to push Force.com cloud development to new heights.

The DreamOps Success Group is a technical forum with a strict focus on development issues. It is not a place for job postings, professional networking, general-interest announcements, or sales and marketing discussions. It's a place where we create and share hardcore technical solutions. If you are ready to get your geek on, join the DreamOps group today!

The DreamOps Manifesto is a prioritized roadmap of Force.com Ideas that we believe will push the platform to new heights, along with a punchlist of existing features most essential to enterprise development. Upvote the ideas and sign the manifesto today!
