What is Salesforce1 Lightning?

[Jesse Altman] You may have heard that Salesforce1 Lightning was announced at Dreamforce ’14. Truthfully, it may have been a bit confusing at first if you were following the development scene beforehand. That confusion may be compounded by the fact that the “Lightning” term seems to be thrown around all over the place. At the end of the day, Lightning is just a term used to described some new functionality. As per Salesforce’s article, “Lightning is the next generation of the Salesforce1 Platform.” It encompasses a large chunk of cool new tools used for extending the platform. – Read more at Jesse Altman
